Acoustic

The Acoustic Guitar is a musical instrument of the string family, that typically has 6 strings but is known to have more even up to 12. The term 'acoustic guitar' usually refers to the metal string version of the instrument which comes in various body shapes and sizes like the Jumbo, Dreadnought, Grand Concert, Grand Auditorium and Parlor.

Acoustic Electric

An acoustic-electric guitar is an acoustic guitar fitted with a microphone or a magnetic or piezoelectric pickup. They are used in a variety of music genres where the sound of an acoustic guitar is desired but more volume is required, especially during live performances. Wikipedia

Classical

The classical guitar is a member of the guitar family used in classical music and other styles. An acoustic wooden string instrument with strings made of gut or nylon, it is a precursor of the modern steel-string acoustic and electric guitars, both of which use metal strings. Wikipedia
